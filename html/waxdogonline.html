Wax Dog Sound Lab

Spring 2024

Introduction

This was a personal project I completed over the course of 4 weeks. WAX DOG is a local musician who was looking to rent out their studio space and needed a website to advertise their services. I was responsible for the artistic direction, photography, and coding of the site. I worked closely with WAX DOG to make sure the design met their needs. The finished site can be found here: http://waxdog.online/

Process

To begin, I visited the studio for a photoshoot. I helped take photos of the various equipment in the studio using my Fujifilm X-Pro1. I later edited these photos in lightroom to improve their contrast and legibility. Below you can see an example of a picture before and after editing.
