Photo Gallery

This collection was taken in New Westminster BC, 2022. I moved to BC recently and decided to explore the area and take some pictures. The fog was so thick this day that the horizon line was invisible. There was only white, glowing fog.

These pictures were taken in Saskatoon Saskatchewan in the summer of 2019. Insects have always fascinated me and I really enjoy exploring the world through a micro lens.

I took these photos in the town I grew up in, Forrest Lake Minnesota, in the year 2020. The heart of these photos is in nostalgia and soft light.

I took these in Invermere, 2022, on 35mm film. I loved learning to shoot film, and theres a softness about the medium that works well with the quietude and stillness of the snow that I really like here.

These photos were taken in Grand Cayman in 2020 for my high school IB art collection. They were inspired by the works of Imogen Cunningham. I learned how to compose in black and white by applying the monochrome filter on my camera while shooting rather than adding it in post.
